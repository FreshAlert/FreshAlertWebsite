About FreshAlert

FreshAlert is an app designed to help users manage their groceries more efficiently. Users can scan the receipts of their groceries to track the items they've purchased along with their expiration dates. The app's primary goal is to prevent food wastage by sending timely alerts when products are nearing their expiration date.
